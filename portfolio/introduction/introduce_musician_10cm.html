10cm (singer)

the South Korean male singer-songwriter
Kwon Jung-yeol, better known by the stage name 10cm, released first album as a solo artist on September 1, 2017.

"10cm is a one-member band under Magic Strawberry Sound and POCLANOS. Originally a duo, they debuted on April 22, 2010 with the release of their first EP The 1st EP. In July 2017, it was announced that Yoon Cheoul Jong would be leaving the band due to health reasons and the expiration of his contract."
